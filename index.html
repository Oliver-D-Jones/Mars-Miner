Martian Miners

The year is 2107 and after hundreds of years of modern living, people of Earth are facing shortages in natural resources. Mars is beginning to be colonized but it is not feasible to transport all 11 billion humans to Mars.

Your goal will be to select a miner who can return as much raw material to back to the people of Earth...
